# Fellowship Comparison Dashboard Context

## Fellowship Program Overview

This dashboard provides comparative analytics across different fellowship cohorts and programs, enabling longitudinal analysis and program effectiveness measurement.

## Key Terms and Concepts

### Fellowship Cohorts
- **Cohort**: A group of fellows who started their fellowship journey at the same time
- **Cohort Comparison**: Analysis comparing outcomes, retention rates, and impact metrics across different fellowship groups
- **Multi-Year Analysis**: Tracking fellowship program evolution and improvement over time

### Comparison Metrics
- **Retention Rate**: Percentage of fellows who complete their full fellowship term
- **School Impact Score**: Aggregated measure of positive changes implemented in partner schools
- **Program Completion Rate**: Fellows who successfully meet all fellowship requirements
- **Post-Fellowship Engagement**: Continued involvement of fellows after program completion

### Geographic and Demographic Analysis
- **Regional Performance**: Comparing fellowship outcomes across different geographic areas
- **Fellow Background Analysis**: Impact of fellow demographics on program success
- **School Type Comparison**: Urban vs rural, government vs private school partnerships

### Program Evolution
- **Year-over-Year Improvement**: Tracking how program enhancements affect outcomes
- **Best Practice Identification**: Identifying most effective fellowship approaches
- **Scaling Insights**: Understanding what works when expanding to new regions

## Data Interpretation Guidelines

### When analyzing comparative data:
- **Cohort Size Impact**: Smaller cohorts may show higher variance in metrics
- **Timeline Considerations**: Different cohorts may be at different stages of completion
- **External Factors**: Economic, political, or social changes affecting different cohorts
- **Program Changes**: Methodology improvements between cohorts should be considered

### Important Caveats
- **Incomplete Data**: Recent cohorts may have incomplete outcome data
- **Selection Bias**: Later cohorts may benefit from improved fellow selection processes
- **Maturation Effects**: Some outcomes only become apparent after fellowship completion
- **Regional Variables**: Geographic differences may affect comparability

## Dashboard Usage Notes

This dashboard is particularly valuable for:
- **Strategic Planning**: Informing future fellowship program design
- **Impact Reporting**: Demonstrating program effectiveness to stakeholders
- **Continuous Improvement**: Identifying areas for program enhancement
- **Resource Allocation**: Understanding where to invest program resources
