# Fellowship School App 25-26 Dashboard Context

## Program Overview
This dashboard tracks the 2025-26 Fellowship Program school application and placement process. The Fellowship Program is BHUMI's flagship 2-year paid fellowship program that develops young leaders (ages 20-30) to drive systemic change in education. Fellows work directly in government schools implementing the Whole School Transformation Programme.

## Key Terminology

**Fellow**: A young leader (ages 20-30) who has been selected for BHUMI's 2-year paid fellowship program. Fellows receive a monthly grant of ₹25,500 and work directly in government schools.

**School Application Process**: The process by which fellows apply to work in specific schools. This involves assessment of school needs, fellow preferences, and optimal matching.

**SFPC (School Fellow Program Cohort)**: The cohort data structure that tracks fellow-school assignments for the 2025-26 academic year.

**School Transformation**: BHUMI's comprehensive approach to improving government schools through direct intervention, focusing on holistic educational development.

## Metrics Explanation

**Student Count**: Total number of students across all schools where fellows are placed. This represents the direct reach of the fellowship program.

**School Count**: Number of distinct schools where fellows have been placed or applied to work. Shows the geographic and institutional reach of the program.

**Fellow Count**: Number of individual fellows participating in the 2025-26 cohort. Each fellow typically works in one primary school.

## Data Interpretation Guidelines

- **Student Impact**: The student count reflects the potential direct impact of the fellowship program. Higher numbers indicate broader reach.
- **School Coverage**: School count shows the diversity of educational settings fellows are exposed to, from urban to rural schools.
- **Fellow Deployment**: Fellow count indicates the human resources deployed for the school transformation initiative.
- **Capacity Planning**: These metrics help in understanding resource allocation and planning for the fellowship program.

## Program Context

**Academic Year**: 2025-26 represents the latest cohort of the fellowship program.

**Selection Process**: Fellows undergo a multi-stage assessment including online application, video submission, and final evaluation with lesson execution, group discussion, and personal interviews.

**Program Goals**:
- Transform schools in the short term through direct intervention
- Develop future education leaders for long-term systemic change
- Create a cadre of changemakers with comprehensive understanding of the education ecosystem

## Important Notes

- Data is tracked through Google Sheets-based systems for comprehensive assessment data
- Fellows receive structured learning, mentorship, and exposure to grassroots organizations, policymakers, and CSR initiatives
- The program emphasizes systems thinking and holistic educational approach
- School placements are strategic decisions based on school needs and fellow capabilities
